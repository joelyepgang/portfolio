Description

big data is the way to study a vast amount of data in order to establish original models that will give us a finer view of reality and allow us to make more relevant decisions. This conference allowed us to understand that it is imperative to have usable data if we want to build relevant decision models. I noticed the problem of the Data Warehouse and the data mining where the quality of the collected data makes all the difference. We have been able to observe that obviously, to store a quantity of data has no other purpose than to draw lessons relevant to increasing our knowledge of our business environment in this case.One of the main interests of Big Data is to take advantage of an impressive amount of data to try to predict behaviors such as fraud or better anticipate the risks this case is more use in machine learning. This conference was very intrusive for me because big data remains an area that I will explore again.
