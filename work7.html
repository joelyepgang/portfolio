Description

EVS is a company specializing in the manufacture of video servers for the recording of professional images on computer media and their processing: instantaneous snapshots, instant replay of images, enrichment of content by adding metadata, broadcasting. Listed in Brussels, it is part of the index Next 150. Well before this visit I always asked the question to know what was really happening during slow motion and how it is done. Gracious ephec I had the opportunity to discover it. During this experience, I noticed that the company is developing and producing its own hardware, notably the XT3 video server and the programs used to control it with Multicam and IPDirector, which also enable high quality slow motion. There are around 5000 operators worldwide (LSM operator) of all nationalities using XT3 and Multicam. on this occasion we were able to test this material. Its equipment has been used on a very large scale during all the last major world sports events. its manufacturing site is located in liege.it was a moving experience
