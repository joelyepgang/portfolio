Description

Colruyt Group supplies its stores from several distribution centers. Each brand (Colruyt, OKay, Bio-Planet, Retail Partners and Solucious), works according to its own assortments, preferences (customers) and forecasts. As a commissioner, I have been working in the hal distribution center for five years and those two weeks and sometimes full time. I collect the products. Together with my colleagues, we prepare orders and make sure they arrive on time in the store or at the customer's. Speech recognition tells us which items to collect (refrigerated and frozen foods, dry foods or toys). In our work, we are precise and attentive to safety: many people are busy in our logistics centers, and it is therefore important that all follow the instructions. we work as part of a two-break system. One week we start in the morning and the other week in the afternoon. In our center we also work on Saturdays. what is interresting is knowing the schedule well in advance, which allows me to organize myself. this job is a pride for me because if I managed to get here last, it's just that it allowed me to be able to meet my first needs and my schooling.
